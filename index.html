How to use myICM 2026

📋 Schedule

Browse all ICM 2026 events. Use the date tabs to see one day (or all days) and the category menu to narrow down to Plenary Lectures, Section Lectures, Short Communications, and more. Tap an event to read its abstract. Tap ＋ to add an event to your calendar (it becomes ✓).

📅 Calendar

Days with a dot have events you picked. Today is outlined. Tap a day to see your events for that day in time order.

⭐ Day view & highlights

Each event shows its time, room, speaker, and title. Tap the star to highlight the events you most don't want to miss. Tap an event to see its abstract again — this works even offline.

✏️ Your own events

In a day view, tap ＋ Add my own event to put anything on your schedule — a lunch, a meeting, a walk to the Liberty Bell. Tap one of your events to edit or delete it.

📱 Install on iPhone

  1. Open this site in Safari: https://cherkis.github.io/myICMschedule2026/
  2. Tap the Share button (square with an arrow).
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen, then Add.

The app opens full-screen from its own icon, like a native app.

🔒 Privacy

All your data stays on this device. Your selections, highlights, and personal events are stored locally in your browser and are never sent anywhere. The app has no accounts, no analytics, and no tracking.

⚠️ Schedule accuracy

This app shows a snapshot of the ICM program. Talks can be moved or cancelled at any time — always verify important events against the current schedule at icm2026.org.
